Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

Information We Collect

HydroFlow is designed with your privacy in mind. The app collects and stores the following data locally on your device: • Water intake records (date, amount, beverage type) • Daily hydration goals and preferences • Mood notes (optional) • Reminder settings • Achievement data All data is stored locally using iOS UserDefaults and is NOT transmitted to any external servers.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to: • Track your daily water intake • Calculate hydration statistics • Send reminder notifications (if enabled) • Display your progress and achievements • Provide personalized insights We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third parties.

Data Storage

All your hydration data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure UserDefaults system. Your data remains on your device and is: • Encrypted by iOS when device is locked • Included in your iCloud/iTunes backup (if enabled) • Never transmitted over the internet • Permanently deleted if you uninstall the app

Notifications

If you enable reminder notifications, we use Apple's local notification system to send you hydration reminders. These notifications: • Are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device • Do not require internet connectivity • Can be disabled at any time in Settings • Do not track your location or behavior

Analytics and Diagnostics

HydroFlow may collect anonymous crash logs and diagnostic information to improve app stability and performance. This data: • Does not contain personally identifiable information • Is used solely for debugging and improvement • Is handled according to Apple's privacy standards You can disable analytics in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.

Apple Health Integration

If you enable Apple Health integration in Settings, HydroFlow will sync your water intake and caffeine intake records to the Health app on your device. • Data synced: Water intake amount, beverage type, and caffeine content • Storage: Stored locally on your device within the Health app • No data is transmitted to external servers through this integration • You can revoke access at any time: Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices

In-App Purchases

HydroFlow offers a one-time Pro upgrade purchase. Purchase transactions are: • Processed securely by Apple through the App Store • Subject to Apple's privacy policy • Not collected or stored by HydroFlow We only verify your purchase status locally through Apple's StoreKit framework.

Intellectual Property & AI-Generated Content

All images, videos, and visual assets used in HydroFlow are: • Created by AI tools under the direction of the app creator • Original works that do not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights • Licensed exclusively for use in HydroFlow • Protected by applicable copyright laws The creator retains all rights to these AI-generated materials. No third-party copyrighted content is used without proper authorization.

Children's Privacy

HydroFlow is suitable for users aged 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require account creation or online connectivity.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data: • Access: All your data is visible within the app • Modify: Edit or delete records at any time • Delete: Uninstalling the app permanently removes all data • Export: Share your data through the app's export feature (if available)
