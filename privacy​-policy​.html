HydroFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

HydroFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

HydroFlow does not collect or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data is stored locally on your device.

  • Hydration data: Your daily water intake records are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to Apple Health.
  • Health data: With your permission, we read and write water intake data to Apple Health (HealthKit). This data never leaves your device or Apple's ecosystem.
  • Notification preferences: Your reminder settings are stored locally on your device.
  • App preferences: Settings such as daily goal, beverage types, and language are stored locally.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

HydroFlow requests optional access to HealthKit to read and write water intake data. This integration is entirely optional. HealthKit data is governed by Apple's own privacy policy and remains within Apple's ecosystem.

In-App Purchases

Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Notifications

With your permission, we send local push notifications to remind you to drink water. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and no data is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

HydroFlow does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
